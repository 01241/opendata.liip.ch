Get your data on opendata.swiss

The Swiss Open Government Data Portal, opendata.swiss, allows both public administrations and organizations with a mandate from the state to open their data. It proposes users extensive search functions (by keyword search or according to a chosen category or organisation).

According to your needs, we support you in publishing your data openly ​on opendata.swiss​, through three different options of the portal.

A

Manual web form

  • Small amount of data
  • Fixed dataset

One-time update by the publisher.

B

XML importer

  • Medium amount of data
  • Frequent updates

Automatised one-time update.

C

Harvester

  • Voluminous datasets
  • Regular updates

Automation of import process.
