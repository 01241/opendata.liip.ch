Open your data with us!

We help swiss public administration publish their data on the Open Gouvernment Data Portal and develop custom web applications for all kind of companies.

The Swiss Open Government Data Portal allows public administrations to open their data and proposes the users extensive search functions (by keyword search or according to a chosen category or organisation).

According to your needs, we support you in publishing your data, through three different options.

A

Manual web form

  • Small amount of data
  • Fixed dataset

One-timed update by the owner.

B

XML importer

  • Medium amount of data
  • Frequent updates

Automatised one-timed update.

C

Harvester

  • Voluminous datasets
  • Automation of import process

Regular automatised updates.
